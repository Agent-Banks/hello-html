Cody's Hobbies

Things I enjoy to do:

1. Working on Cars

The Story

When I bought my first car, I put so much time and effort into fixing it up and making it better than what it looked like when it came out of the factory. My first car was a 2001 Mitsubishi Eclipse V6 manual Spyder GT. I wanted to have a stick shift as my first car, because I felt like if I did not learn now, I would never learn. I was surprised to figure out that stick shift was not to hard to learn!

The Work

I have to admit I put way to much money into a car that was only worth $3,000, but I loved working on it. At first, before wanting to become a Software Developer, I wanted to be a mechanic. This changed shortly after I realized that I hated being covered in grease and oil all day. Some of the work I completed is listed below:

  1. Switching out the stock headlights with aftermarket LED lights
  2. Switching out the stock fog lights with aftermarket LED lights
  3. Painting some of the ugly interior parts black to match the color of the car
  4. Installing an aftermarket radio
  5. Re-painting the car black
  6. Painting the rims black to match the color of the car
  7. Removing Stock suspension and replaced with coilovers

The Downfall

Sadly, like most first cars, it eventually died :(

The clutch burned out after 150,000 miles and I could no longer drive the car around. The story about where this happened is one to be told for another day.
